Pelle Van Gheluwe

Biography

Pelle Van Gheluwe (Ghent, August 9, 1997) is a Belgian artist, known for his paintings, drawings and spatial work. He currently lives and works in Ghent, after moving from the village of Scheldewindeke earlier this year. He has a girlfriend and two brothers. He loves his four-feeted companion, Boris the dog. He studies painting at the Luca School of Arts in Ghent. 

Pelle's oeuvre currently consists out of several figurative works, as well as many recent abstract paintings, based on human emotion and layeredness.

His work is inspired by the abstract expressionists, such as Willem De Kooning, Mark Rothko, Franz Kline and Clyfford Still.
